Features

Everything you need for remote operation

📻

Full Rig Control

Frequency, mode, bandwidth, VFO, split, PTT, and raw command access through Hamlib's rigctld. Support for 200+ radio models.

🎧

Bidirectional Audio

Low-latency Opus-compressed audio streaming. Hear the radio and transmit with your mic from any browser on your network.

📹

Live Video

Browser-native H.264 video streaming from the Electron host to remote clients. See your radio's front panel in real time.

📈

Spectrum Scope

Live waterfall and spectrum display via Hamlib UDP multicast (Icom) or direct USB-SPI from the Yaesu FT-710's built-in FT4222H chip.

🔑

CW Keyer & Decoder

Server-side iambic keyer (A/B/straight) with client-side sidetone. GGMorse WASM decoder displays received CW text in real time.

📍

POTA / SOTA / WWFF Spots

Live activator spots with band, mode, and age filtering. Click-to-tune jumps your VFO directly to spotted frequencies.

☀️

Solar & Propagation

HF band conditions, VHF phenomena, SFI, SSN, and MUF map from hamqsl.com and prop.kc2g.com. Know what's open before you tune.

💻

Configurable Layout

Drag-and-drop panel grid that adapts from desktop to phone. Add, remove, and rearrange panels to build your operating view.

🔒

Secure by Default

Auto-generated HTTPS with EC P-256 certificates covering localhost and all LAN IPs. No manual TLS setup required.
